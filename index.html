Game Instructions

PC Controls:

  • Use arrow keys to move the snake.

Mobile Controls:

  • Swipe up, down, left, or right to move the snake.

Level Selection:

  • Click on the Level 1, Level 2, or Level 3 buttons to adjust the speed.
